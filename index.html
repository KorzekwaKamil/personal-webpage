Kamil Korzekwa

About me

Present: I am currently a Research Fellow in Quantum Information Theory working in Quantum Science Research Group at the University of Sydney, Australia.

Past: I come from Kluczbork, a small town in south-western Poland. I got my Bachelor's degree in Physics and Master's degree in Nanoengineering from Wrocław University of Science and Technology, where I started my research with Professor Paweł Machnikowski. I then joined Centre for Doctoral Training on Controlled Quantum Dynamics at Imperial College London, where I pursued my PhD degree in Physics under the joint supervision of Professor Terry Rudolph and Dr David Jennings.

More: On this website you can find details concerning my research, look at my up-to-date CV, or check what I do beyond physics.
